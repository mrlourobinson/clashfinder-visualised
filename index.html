How to get your Clashfinder data:

Visit the Clashfinder page for your event - this tool has been built with Glastonbury 2024 in mind, so unsure if this will work with other events.

Once you have completed your Clashfinder, at the top select Data then Export. Choose Your Highlights and set the format to CSV and click OK.

Now you have got your file, upload it below and see how many clashes you have!
